Culture & Food

Maharashtra has a rich culture with festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi and traditional food such as Vada Pav, Misal Pav and Puran Poli.

Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti 🚩

Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti celebrates the birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the legendary founder of the Maratha Empire. Across Maharashtra people organize grand processions, traditional dhol performances and cultural events to honor his bravery, leadership and vision of Swarajya.

Gudi Padwa

Gudi Padwa marks the Maharashtrian New Year and the beginning of spring. Families raise a decorated "Gudi" outside their homes as a symbol of victory and prosperity and prepare festive foods like Puran Poli and Shrikhand.

Pola / Bail Pola

Bail Pola is a festival dedicated to farmers and their bulls. The animals are decorated with ornaments and garlands and are honored for their importance in agriculture and rural life.

Narali Pournima

Narali Pournima is celebrated by fishing communities. Fishermen offer coconuts to the sea god Varuna and pray for safety and prosperity before starting the fishing season after the monsoon.

Nag Panchami

Nag Panchami is a traditional festival where snakes are worshipped as symbols of protection and fertility. Devotees offer milk and prayers and celebrate the festival with rituals and devotion.

Janmashtami

Janmashtami celebrates the birth of Lord Krishna. In Maharashtra the famous "Dahi Handi" event takes place where teams form human pyramids to break a pot filled with curd.

Travel & Transport Details

✈️ By Air

Maharashtra has major airports in Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur and Aurangabad. Regular domestic and international flights are available.

🚆 By Train

Indian Railways connects Maharashtra with all major cities. Important stations include Mumbai CST, Pune Junction and Nagpur.

🚌 By Road

State transport buses (MSRTC) and private buses operate across cities, towns and tourist destinations.

🚕 Local Transport

Auto-rickshaws, taxis, local trains and metro services are available in major cities like Mumbai and Pune.

🚗 Car Rentals

Self-drive and chauffeur-driven cars are available for tourists through local agencies and online platforms.

📌 Travel Tips

Book transport in advance during peak seasons and prefer local guides for forts and trekking routes.
