Coastal escapes – Beaches

Alibaug popular

Close to Mumbai, Alibaug offers a blend of Portuguese forts, clean shores and water sports. Perfect for a weekend getaway.

⚓ jet skiing 🏖️ beach shacks
Tarkarli scuba diving

Crystal clear waters and coral reefs make Tarkarli a hotspot for snorkeling and scuba diving. Also known for its backwaters.

🤿 scuba 🛶 kayaking
Ganpatipule

Famous for the Swayambhu Ganesh temple right on the shore. Calm waters and white sand stretch for miles.

🙏 temple visit 🐚 shell museum
Other pristine beaches: Diveagar, Harihareshwar, Velas (turtle nesting), Murud, Kashid – each with unique charm.

Cool retreats – Hill stations

Mahabaleshwar

Crown of the Sahyadri, famous for strawberry farms, panoramic points and ancient temples. Boating at Venna lake is a must.

🍓 strawberry 🔭 sunset point
Lonavala & Khandala

Lush green valleys, waterfalls and the famous Bhaja caves. Chowk point and Duke's nose offer spectacular views.

🌧️ monsoon magic 🍬 chikki
Matheran

Asia's only automobile-free hill station. Toy train, red laterite paths and 38 viewpoints – a walker's paradise.

🚂 toy train 🚫 no vehicles

Western Ghats – Ghats & passes

The majestic escarpments of the Sahyadri are crisscrossed with ancient ghats (mountain passes) that offer thrilling drives and breathtaking scenery.

Amboli Ghat

Southern Maharashtra's rain country. Known for waterfalls, mist, and rare biodiversity.

Bhor Ghat

Railway and road artery between Mumbai and Pune. Ancient caravan route with 80+ rail tunnels.

Kumbharli Ghat

Connects Chiplun to Karad, flanked by waterfalls and the scenic Koyna wildlife.

Tamhini Ghat

A monsoon jewel, with countless waterfalls and winding roads through thick forests.

🌿 Malshej Ghat ⛰️ Varandha Ghat 🏞️ Kasara Ghat 🌋 Amba Ghat

Adrenaline rush – adventure hubs

Kundalika River Rafting

One of the few year-round rafting destinations in India. Grade II+ rapids near Kolad, 100 km from Mumbai.

🛶 rafting 🏕️ riverside camping
Kamshet Paragliding

Thermal heaven for paragliders. Tandem flights and training amid lush hills, 110 km from Mumbai.

🪂 paragliding 📸 aerial views
Sahyadri trekking

Hundreds of forts & hills – Rajmachi, Harishchandragad, Kalavantin – offer epic treks for all levels.

🥾 night trek ⛰️ fort climb
Wildlife & water: Scuba diving at Malvan (Sindhudurg) , tiger safari at Tadoba, birding at Bhimashankar & Narnala.
